DATA ANALYST OVERVIEW
SKILLS
  • Data Tools

    • Proficient in Excel, SQL, and data visualization tools like POWER BI.

  • Programming Languages

    • Knowledge of Python or R for data manipulation and analysis.

  • Statistical Knowledge

    • Understanding of deceptive statistics, hypothesis testing and predictive modeling.

  • Data Cleaning

    • Skilled in data preparation and cleaning to ensure accuracy.

  • Communication

    • Ability to translate complex data insights into simple, actionable reports for non-technical stakeholders.

  • Attention to Detail

    • Ensuring high accuracy when analyzing and interpreting data.

QUALIFICATIONS
  • Education

    • Bachelor's degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, or in a related field.

  • Certifications

    • Optional certifications such as Google Data Analytics, Professional Certificate, IBM Data Science Professional Certificate.

  • Experience

    • Entry-level positions may require internships or volunteer work involving data entry, analysis and reporting.
JOB RESPONSIBILITIES
  • Collect, clean, and analyze large datasets to uncover trends and insights.

  • Build interactive dashboards and reports to support decision-making.

  • Collaborate with teams to ensure data integrity and availability.

  • Present findings to key stakeholders and provide data-driven recommendations.
